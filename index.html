Who we are

Om Shree Mahalakshmi Transport is committed to the cost optimization method in providing competitive logistics solutions Since one decades of service Om Shree Mahalakshmi Transport has been a influential in providing innovative and value added service to the automotive industries. It was founded by Mr. M.Mohan Kumar with the co operation of Mr. M.Mageshwaran in 2013. As the organisation was developing itself, the name was coined into Om ShreeMahalakshmiTransport. it assures the customer satisfaction which is ultimate aim our organisation.
